PROP

Prop is a state level inter collegiate Project Open House Panorama hosted by RNSIT every year. It facilitates learning and career enhancement research and exploring of ideas in all categories of Computer Science and Information Technology. It gives an opportunity for companies to identify individuals with exceptional capabilities to develop functional projects.

OpenDesign

Open Design is a one of a kind graphic design competition aimed to test your creative abilities. It is the first time such a competition is being held at RNSIT, which will judge your designing abilities with a freedom of choosing your software platform. A concept, preferably social, will be provided to you on the spot and you will have to design your poster accordingly.

Register here

Code wars

CodeWars is a coding contest, designed exclusively for those with a knack of problem solving. The problem statements provided will be level based and the participant will be eligible for the next level only on clearing the preceding level. There is no language constraint for the ease of the participants. The winning team might get a chance to be mentored by the best in the industry in the form of an internship opportunity, apart from the prize money.

Register here

Prastuthi

Prasthuthi is a state level Inter-Collegiate paper presentation which attempts to encourage the efforts of those who would like to "Ideate" for a better future. For those who wish to voice their innovative ideas in an effective manner, this provides an opportunity to network, compete and grow with the best.
